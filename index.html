About Us

At Jos Car Care, we are deeply committed to delivering top-tier automotive detailing and maintenance services. Our service spectrum is vast, covering all aspects of car care that ranges from essential maintenance to advanced, specialized treatments.

Our expert team excels in a broad selection of services including Ceramic Coating, Graphene Coating, PPF Patchwork Painting, Polishing, Teflon Coating, Under Coating, and Glass Coating. We understand the importance of a well-maintained interior and offer thorough interior cleaning to ensure your comfort while driving. Notably, we also offer a unique service of touch and polish work for test drives, making sure that your vehicle is in its best shape when it matters the most.

Our service does not end at our center, for we also provide on-the-spot checking within a radius of 15 km. We understand the importance of convenience in today's fast-paced world and aim to make your car care experience as hassle-free and efficient as possible.

In our commitment to excellence, we continuously strive to elevate the standards of automotive care. Our skilled professionals, cutting-edge equipment, and customer-focused approach set us apart. When you choose Jos Car Care, you're not just choosing a service - you're choosing a partner who cares for your vehicle as much as you do.
